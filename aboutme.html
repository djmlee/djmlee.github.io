    About Me

    On the days that I am not doing work, you can find me doing a LOT of tinkering (whether it would be for code/cars/motorcycles/bikes/skateboards).

    I am definitely no stranger to projects. I love it when something breaks and then I find myself learning about how something works to make it better.

    I enjoy a top-down cruise in my 1991 Mazda Miata or on my 2011 Tomos Streetmate R. You might find me in my friend's garage learning CAD to 3D print a battery container for my custom built electric skateboard, programming something silly to illustrate a new concept, learning how to thermally mold Kydex in my buddy's garage, cleaning the carburetor on my bike.

    I believe that we are born into a life of learning. If a person is not learning - there is no growth.

    Without growth, what is there to aspire to?
